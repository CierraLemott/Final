About Me

Photographer & Journalist

My name is Cierra Lemott. I'm a junior at Columbia College Chicago majoring in journalism with minors in photography and social media & digital strategy. I was introduced to journalism through True Star Media's editorial program via After School Matters during my sophomore year of high school when I attended Brooks College Prep. I currently work for True Star Media as Lead Reporter and Communications Assistant. I also work for the Columbia Chronicle as a Multimedia Reporter. In addition to my contribution to these two publications, I have my own blog titled Kodak's Corner, which is my corner of the Internet where I'm free to express my thoughts on topics that interest me. Journalism and photography have given me an outlet and a voice to represent those who are often silenced.

My Services

Photography

I've been taking pictures for as long as I can remember. My mom is a huge contributor toward this as she always had a camera in hand, capturing memories. I decided to explore the world of photojournalism as a way to cater to both my love of photography and my love of journalism.

Journalism

My journalism career began in the summer of 2017 when I was a part of True Star Media's editorial team for the first time. From there, I moved from working only during the After School Matters sessions to working directly for True Star year-round as a freelance journalist. I've gone on to have hundreds of published works on True Star's blog which can be found here. In the summer of 2021, I made a blog called Kodak's Corner where I write about things that are more personal to me. I began working for the Columbia Chronicle as a staff reporter in the fall of 2022 where I've published some written works that can be viewed here.
